YOUTUBE MIXER

How to Use YouTube Mixer

  1. Search (Optional): Use the search bar to find YouTube videos. Click 'Search' to open results in a new tab.
  2. Load Videos: Paste the full YouTube video URL (e.g., https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...) into the 'Enter YouTube Link' fields for Video 1 and Video 2.
  3. Click Load: Press the 'Load' button next to each input field to load the video into its player.
  4. Playback Controls: Use the 'Play', 'Pause', and 'Stop' buttons beneath each video player to control playback individually.
  5. Mix Audio: Use the 'Audio Mixer Fader' slider at the bottom.
    • Slide LEFT to increase the volume of Video 1 and decrease Video 2.
    • Slide RIGHT to increase the volume of Video 2 and decrease Video 1.
    • The MIDDLE position balances the audio equally.

Enjoy mixing your videos!
